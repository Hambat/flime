How it works?

  • Invite your freelance team
    Send a single email to your freelancer or upload a CSV file to send a bulky invitation. After agreeing with the public offer and ID verification, your freelancer then becomes a subcontractor of Flime. More precisely, Flime acts as your general contractor.
  • Set the task
    Type a detailed description, indicate the cost of the work and tick a box to get the IP rights for the result (if needed). This information allows us to generate proper accounting documents for you. Upload a CSV file for a bulky task list to save even more time.
  • Freelancer ID verification
    Freelancers have to pass a KYC procedure before they start working on your tasks to verify their identity. This gives us information we need to keep your relationships compliant according to yours and your freelancer’s local tax regimes.
  • Balance Top-Up
    To accept the result of the work you have to first top-up your balance in Flime via bank transfer. Specify the amount, download the invoice, and transfer the money from your corporate bank account. The invoice is paid according to the contract you sign with Flime during the registration.
  • Make a payment
    When the job has been done and your balance is replenished, just click the «pay» button to send the money to the freelancer.
Get all the papers you need
At the beginning of the next month, you will receive reports for the previous period straight into your Flime account. This is available to download and print if needed.
